Chicken with Sweetcorn and Bell Pepper

Description

Chicken with Sweetcorn and Bell Pepper is an easy one pot dish. It is suitable for people with low cooking skills or very busy people who want to fill their stomachs quickly.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • Paprika
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • 150g can of sweetcorn
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • A side dish based on preference

Instructions

  1. We finely chop the onion and put into a large sauce pan with olive oil on medium heat.
  2. We dice the chicken breasts and when the onion has turned glassy we add the chicken into the sauce pan.
  3. We wait a couple of minutes for the chicken to get cooked from the outside and then add salt, paprika and pepper.
  4. Then, we add two glasses of water into the sauce pan and let the meat steam on medium heat until the water evaporates.
  5. Just before the water has evaporated we add the sweetcorn and sliced bell pepper.
  6. We cook the side dish and serve immediately.
