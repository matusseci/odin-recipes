Avocado and Egg Toast

Description

Avocado and Egg Toast is an excellent dish to get your day started as it contains all the macronutrients and will provide you enough energy for the whole morning.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium avocado
  • 2 toasts
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. We mix the eggs in a bowl, salt them and then fry them on a medium heat.
  2. In the meantime, we spoon out the avocado and mix with a pinch of salt and pepper until we get a spread.
  3. We put the avocado spread on the toasts and serve with the warm scrambled eggs.
