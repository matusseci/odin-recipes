Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a simple Italian dish containing white sauce and pancetta. It is suitable for people with low cooking skills or very busy people who want to fill their stomachs quickly.

Ingredients

  • 100g spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 50g pancetta
  • 80g carbonara sauce
  • 30g Parmesan, grated
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. We put about 1l of water into a sauce pan and salt thoroughly.
  2. Once the water starts boiling we add spaghetti and cook for 12 minutes.
  3. In the meantime, we warm up the carbonara sauce in a separate sauce pan and fry the pancetta on a fryin pan.
  4. When the carbonara sauce is warm, we add the rated Parmesan and fried pancetta.
  5. Once the pasta has cooked we strain the water and add the olive oil.
  6. Lastly, we mix the pasta with the sauce and serve immediately.
